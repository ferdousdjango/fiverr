EHJ & SJ Consultancy are proud to be accredited by the IASME Consortium for achieving Cyber Essentials Registration No: IASME-CE-017466, demonstrating our commitment to not only providing security services but as an organisation we tackle our own security with the same approach as we do for our Clients.

To ensure we meet the higest standards in our Industry sector, we are certified by IASME Registration No: IASME-SA-016593 to have obtained the self-verification of the IASME Governance Standard. This ensures we have an information security maangeemnt system in place including a GDPR management system in place.

We are proud to be part of the Crown Commercial Services and are a registered Supplier for the following frameworks, whereby Public Sector Customers can engage with us knowing we are certified by the Governments CCS Framework Standards to be an approved Crown Commercial Supplier.

We are proud to be a member of the Cyber Exchange to ensure we actively engage and obtain the latest news and events within our Sector to stay abreadth of the current trends in Cyber Security and to extend our knowledge network.
